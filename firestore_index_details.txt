Here are the complete details for creating the Firestore index manually:

1.  **Collection ID**: 
    users

2.  **Fields to Index**:
    - Field Path: role
    - Order / Index Type: Ascending

3.  **Query Scope**:
    Collection

---
**Summary of steps in Firebase Console:**
1. Go to Firestore Database > Indexes > Composite Indexes.
2. Click "Create Index".
3. For "Collection ID", enter: users
4. Under "Fields to Index", enter "role" for the Field Path and select "Ascending".
5. For "Query Scope", ensure "Collection" is selected.
6. Click "Create Index".
